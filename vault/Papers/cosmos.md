# Cosmos World Foundation Model Platform for Physical AI

> An open platform of large pre-trained video world foundation models (diffusion and autoregressive) plus tokenizers and data pipelines, meant to be fine-tuned into physical-AI world models for robotics and driving.

Cosmos releases a suite of world foundation models trained on ~20M hours of physical-world video, in both diffusion and autoregressive-transformer variants, alongside efficient continuous and discrete video tokenizers and a curated data pipeline. The models generate future video conditioned on text, images, and actions and are designed as pre-trained bases that downstream users fine-tune for driving, manipulation, and other embodied tasks. It packages generative video world modeling as reusable open infrastructure for Physical AI.

**Benchmarks:** Physical-AI video benchmarks (driving, manipulation)
**Headline result:** Trained on ~20M hours of video; multiple open model sizes released.
