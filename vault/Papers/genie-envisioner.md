# Genie Envisioner: A Unified World Foundation Platform for Robotic Manipulation

> A unified platform coupling a video world model, a policy head, and an evaluation simulator so manipulation policies can be trained and assessed inside learned imagination.

Genie Envisioner integrates an instruction-conditioned video world model with an action policy and a benchmarking simulator into one closed-loop stack for robotic manipulation. The world model provides visual futures used both to train the policy and to evaluate it without real-robot rollouts, unifying generation, control, and evaluation. It exemplifies the move toward embodied 'world foundation platforms' that serve as reusable neural simulators for manipulation learning.

**Benchmarks:** AgiBot manipulation suite
