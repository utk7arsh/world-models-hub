# The Free-Energy Principle: A Unified Brain Theory?

> Proposes that brains minimize variational free energy, casting perception, learning, and action as inference and prediction-error minimization over an internal model of the world.

Argues that any self-organizing agent resisting disorder must minimize the surprise (variational free energy) of its sensory inputs, which decomposes into perception (updating an internal generative model) and active inference (acting to make sensations match predictions). It unifies predictive coding, the Bayesian brain, and optimal control under one objective, and is a core theoretical reference for biologically motivated world models.
