# Foundations & Theory

> The intellectual bedrock where planning is revealed as learning from imagined data and the brain is cast as a hierarchical generative predictor driven by curiosity and free-energy minimization.

## Papers
- [[schmidhuber-predictive]] — *1990* — The original recurrent 'world model + controller' system, where a predictive network learns environment dynamics and a controller backpropagates through it to plan.
- [[dyna]] — *1991* — The foundational blueprint for model-based RL: interleave real experience, learning a predictive model, and planning against that model with the same update rule.
- [[rao-ballard-pc]] — *1999* — The influential neuroscience model proposing the cortex is a hierarchical generative predictor that only transmits prediction errors upward.
- [[schmidhuber-fun-theory]] — *2010* — A unifying theory casting curiosity and creativity as intrinsic reward for improving a predictive/compressive world model.
- [[friston-fep]] — *2010* — Proposes that brains minimize variational free energy, casting perception, learning, and action as inference and prediction-error minimization over an internal model of the world.
