# Do we need explicit models and planning, or will scaled video generation yield world models for free?

[[sora-world-simulator]] and [[cosmos]] bet that scaling generative video produces emergent world simulation — physics, permanence, 3D — with no explicit dynamics or planner. The structured-planning tradition, from [[muzero]] to [[dreamerv3]], instead builds compact models tuned for decision making and searches or imagines within them. Are the emergent capabilities of giant video models genuine causal understanding usable for control, or plausible-looking correlations that break under intervention? The crux is whether raw scale can substitute for the inductive biases that made planning-based world models work.
